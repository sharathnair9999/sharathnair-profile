30th Sept. 2021

My take on neoG.

I'm pretty sure if you are here, there is no chance you don't know what is neog. But still if you don't know. Let me tell you very simply. It's an initiative by Tanay Pratap

Basically neog doesn't care what's your age, where you are from and what you did till date. If you are hungry to learn and improve your skillset exponentially, this is the best place.

But wait! Is it that simple?

neoG is not easy obviously. How do I know this even before joining? Because it's not pay and take the course. Your hunger and grit to learn is tested even before you get into neoG.

levelOne is paid bootcamp. Now why is it paid?

Because I personally feel it is the fine we are paying on us for not being a self-taught programmer till now and it's completely fine. I personally wasted my parents' money entering into a Tier-3 college by paying donation.

Does Tanay teach any Ninja technique of becoming a self-taught programmer?

I can't answer this accurately. But the community he's trying to build is not just like another community. It's a family. Don't believe me. Go check out the Discord server. Here you find like minded people, highly skilled people than you, more important than that, most helpful people, no matter if you are a noob or pro. So that community itself is a driving force for you to push your limits forward. It's complete online bootcamp. But just imagine if it was offline what would be the amount of transformation we'd be going through if we could interact personally with all the neoGrammars.
